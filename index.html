The Collective

A community for music lovers, record collectors, and hi-fi enthusiasts in South Queensferry.

The Queensferry Music Collective (QMC) was founded on a simple principle: music is a shared experience. Whether you’re a lifelong crate digger, a casual listener, or just looking for a better soundtrack to your night, you’re welcome here.

We host regular meetups at Los Hermanos Pícaros to spin vinyl, talk gear and gigs, and swap recommendations. No pretension—just great tunes and local community.
